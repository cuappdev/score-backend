BoxScoreEntryType
Object

Represents an individual entry in the box score of a game.

Attributes: - team : The team involved in the scoring event. - period : The period or inning of the event. - time : The time of the scoring event. - description : A description of the play or scoring event. - scorer : The name of the scorer. - assist : The name of the assisting player. - score_by : Indicates which team scored. - cor_score : Cornell's score at the time of the event. - opp_score : Opponent's score at the time of the event.
